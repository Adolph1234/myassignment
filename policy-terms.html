Livestock Insurance Policy Terms

1. Coverage Overview

Our livestock insurance policy provides financial protection against unexpected losses due to:

  • Death or injury of insured livestock caused by accidents or diseases.
  • Theft or loss due to natural disasters such as floods, fires, or storms.
  • Compensation for veterinary expenses related to covered incidents.

2. Policy Exclusions

The following are excluded from coverage:

  • Losses due to negligence or lack of proper animal care.
  • Pre-existing health conditions not disclosed at the time of policy purchase.
  • Intentional harm or illegal activities.
  • Losses caused by war, riots, or government confiscation.

3. Premium and Payment Terms

Premiums are calculated based on the number, type, and value of insured livestock. Payments must be made annually or as per the agreed policy schedule to maintain coverage.

Failure to pay premiums on time may result in policy cancellation or denial of claims.

4. Claim Procedure

In the event of a loss, the policyholder must:

  • Notify the insurer within 48 hours of the incident.
  • Provide all necessary documentation, including veterinary reports, police reports (if applicable), and photographs.
  • Allow the insurer or their representatives to inspect the livestock and verify the claim.
  • Submit the official claim form within 14 days of the incident.

5. Claim Approval and Payout

Claims will be reviewed promptly. Approved claims will result in payout according to policy terms, usually within 30 days of approval. The payout amount may be subject to deductibles or limits specified in the policy agreement.

6. Policy Renewal

Policies must be renewed annually. The insurer may revise premiums or terms at the time of renewal based on risk assessments.
